About the website

Our topic is information about sharks. Our website will help people learn what all sharks have in common but also help people identify different sharks by their unique appearances. It will also explain how some sharks adapted to their environment over millions of years. The main goal of our website is to give you a better understanding of sharks and to learn something you didn't know prior to visiting the website.
